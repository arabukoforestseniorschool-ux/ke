Welcome to Arabuko Forest Senior School

Founded in 2010 as a visionary project of Direct Aid International, Arabuko Forest Senior School is a beacon of hope, providing high-quality secondary education. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds, instilling spiritual discipline, and fostering talent to create the responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Our CBC Pathways

Arabuko Forest Senior School offers Two specialised pathways designed to nurture diverse talents and prepare students for their future careers.

Science & Tech

STEM Pathway

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

Ideally suited for students with a strong aptitude for sciences and mathematics. This pathway prepares learners for careers in engineering, medicine, computer science, and technical fields through rigorous practical and theoretical engagement.

Humanities

Social Sciences

Humanities, Languages, and Business Studies.

Designed for students interested in understanding human society and relationships. This pathway focuses on subjects like History, Geography, Religious Education, Languages, and Business, paving the way for careers in law, administration, and education.

Holistic Support

All our pathways are supported by a robust Guidance & Counselling department to ensure every student navigates their academic journey with emotional resilience and clear career goals.
