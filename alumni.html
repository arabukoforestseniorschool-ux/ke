A Legacy of Excellence

Since our inception in 2010, Arabuko Forest Senior School has nurtured young minds into responsible citizens and leaders. The Arabuko Old Boys Association seeks to maintain the bonds of brotherhood formed within our classrooms and dormitories.

Whether you were part of the pioneer class or recently graduated, you remain a vital part of our history. This platform is designed to help you reconnect with former classmates, network with fellow professionals, and give back to the institution that shaped your foundation.
